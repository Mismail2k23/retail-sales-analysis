RETAIL SALES ANALYSIS


Analysis of retail sales data to understand business performance and identify trends.

ABOUT

This project analyzes retail transactions including sales, profits, product categories, and customer segments.

DATASET

- Multi-year retail sales transactions
- Product categories and sub-categories
- Geographic data across regions and states
- Customer segments
- Profit and discount information

ANALYSIS PERFORMED

1. Business performance metrics
2. Category and sub-category analysis
3. Regional sales distribution
4. Customer segment comparison
5. Monthly and quarterly trends

KEY FINDINGS

- Technology products generate highest revenue
- Copiers have strongest profit margins
- West region accounts for largest sales
- Corporate customers biggest segment
- Sales peak in fourth quarter

TOOLS

Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, Jupyter Notebook

FILES

- retail_sales_analysis.ipynb (main analysis)
- PNG files (visualizations)
- CSV files (data)
